Dr. Milind U. Nemade, affectionately known as Pookie Nemu, serves as the Head of the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI-DS). A compassionate leader and an inspiring mentor, he’s beloved by both students and faculty for his approachable demeanor and unwavering dedication to everyone’s success.

Renowned for his groundbreaking work in Speech Processing, Pookie’s research continues to motivate and challenge students to reach new academic heights. His passion for innovation and excellence is evident in all his projects and collaborations.

Beyond his academic accomplishments, Pookie Nemu is known for his deep commitment to the personal and professional growth of his students. His humble nature and selflessness have made him a true role model in the department. Students regularly express their appreciation by organizing birthday celebrations, gifting thoughtful presents, and inviting him to various student-led events.
